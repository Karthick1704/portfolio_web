About Me

My Story So Far

It started with curiosity — the kind that makes you dismantle things just to see how they work. I began my journey in mechanical engineering at Francis Xavier Engineering College, but it wasn't gears and pistons that captured my imagination — it was the invisible architecture of cloud systems and the elegance of code.

After graduating with an 8.42 CGPA, I joined Tata Consultancy Services, where I rapidly evolved from an Assistant Systems Engineer Trainee into a Linux System Administrator managing Azure and AWS infrastructure. I spent nearly three years mastering the craft of DevOps — building CI/CD pipelines, orchestrating containers with Kubernetes, and automating everything with Bash and Terraform.

Today, as a Software Application Engineer II at Amazon, I architect systems that power cross-regional flexibility for Amazon Stores, slashing latency and resolving business-critical incidents at scale. But engineering systems is only half the story — I'm also a passionate UI/UX designer with a Dribbble portfolio, because I believe great engineers should also have great taste.
